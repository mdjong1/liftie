Written Report

Written Report for 12/19/19

It's looking and feeling very wintry out there today! After receiving 6 inches of light, fluffy snow, some sporadic, heavy squalling made its way through the region last night as temperatures plummeted. Did you get the emergency broadcast? Our Snowmaking crew is taking advantage and doing some great work on Red Hat, Recoil, Gunsmoke, and Ramrod Race Trail. Watch for dropping ropes over the next few days. All hats are 35% off today at Gunstock Ski & Sport, be sure to pop in and check out their wide selection!

Tubing & Coaster

The Tubing Hill will be open from 4-8pm, Mountain Coaster is closed on Thursdays.

Terrain Parks

  • Blundersmoke will be open with small, medium, and large features (rails, boxes, snow features)
  • Pistol will be open with small and medium features (rails, boxes, snow feature)

Outdoor Center 

The XC trails are closed until further notice. See the Nordic Report for additional details or call the Outdoor Center with questions. Yurt Yoga begins this weekend!

Resort Notes

  • Night Sessions and Tubing are in full force Tuesday - Saturday 

  • Ski & Sport is hosting 12 Days of Deals

  • Remember to register this week for Kid's Development Teams and Adult Race Leagues.

  • Saturday is Belknap County Day and you can Ski with Santa from 11am - 12:30pm 

Click here for a jpeg of our new Trail Map. We are working on integrating a new updated, interactive map that will be available soon. Stay tuned!

~ Jen
